Proposed Solutions

Problem 1

1. Provision of adequate water.
2. Maintaining proper quantity of moisture.

Solution 1

1. Indoor Hydration System
2. Using Soil Moisture Sensor to sense the moisture and change it accordingly
3. Readings from sensor will be taken by a ECU


Problem 2

1. Ambient Temperature.
2. The temperature required by plant may not be the same as that of surrounding.

Sloution 2

1. Indoor Temperature System
2. Temperature can be sensed using a DHT module.
3. Reading will be taken from sensor using a ECU
4. And it will be variated by sprinkling hot/cold water over the plant and soil according to the need.
5. For sprinkling hot water, the induction coil in the pipe carrying water will be heated



Problem 3

Sunshine

Slotion 3

1. Replicated Sunlight(Full spectrum light)
2. Its intensity and colour can be variated by using its wifi mobile application.
3. The needed intensity of light will be measured by a LDR sensor



Problem 4

Growing indigenous plants on the best possible Indian soil

Solution 4

1. Data set of available soil can be compared with the data set of soil on which the plant actually grows
2. This can suggest the required changes to be made in the available soil.
